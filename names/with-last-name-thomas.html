Phonetic tips for Thomas

Since Thomas starts with a consonant, first names that end in a vowel (e.g. Emma, Olivia, Noah) tend to flow well and create a clear break between first and last name.

Thomas has 2 syllables. First names with a similar syllable count (or within one) often sound balanced when paired with it.
